Duotone Multifold Brochure

Designed and output a two sided multifold brochure. The trim size had to be no larger than A3. The client for the brochure was a new local art school. I was free to decide what the purpose of the brochure would be, who the target audience was and what message I wished to convey. I was required to name the company and design a logo for it. I needed to use imagery, typography, colour and supporting design elements that would help convey the message in the brochure and appeal to my chosen target audience. It needed to be a 2 Pantone spot colour brochure. The final folded size of the brochure was my choice but it had to use a minimum of 2 folds and it needed to include a diecut.
