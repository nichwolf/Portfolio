CD/DVD Custom Packaging

Designed and output a CD/DVD custom package for a client of my choice. I was free to decide what the purpose of the package would be, who the target audience was and what message I wished to convey. The CD/DVD custom package to be designed needed to include at least 2 discs with labels, a minimum 4 page insert /booklet or a minimum of 4 panels on the package, at least 2 pages or 2 panels needed to have sufficient text on them to demonstrate the use of paragraph styles and an EAN13 standard barcode. The CD/DVD custom package was to be full colour process. Dielines were required for the CD/DVD labels and packaging (and wherever else a diecut was used). The final size of my CD/DVD custom package was my choice. I needed to use imagery, typography, colour and supporting design elements that would help convey the message in my CD/DVD custom package and appeal to my chosen target audience.
