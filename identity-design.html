Identity Design

Designed and produced a 2 spot colour identity for a new business of my choice. I was required to name the company and design a vector logo for it. I had to design and output a house style for a letterhead, receipt, tax invoice, compliment slip and business card. I had to also design a simplified style guide which reflected and explained the house style. The letterhead and style guide had to be A4 in size. I could choose the size of the other documents, keeping in mind their individual function and purpose.
