Contemporary Magazine

Designed and output a new contemporary magazine and created a name for it. It needed to have at least 12 pages. It needed to include a contents page or spread. It needed to include a half page advert designed by me. The magazine had to be full colour process (CMYK). The front cover had to include an additional special ink. The final size of the magazine was my choice. Page numbering and the use of master pages were required. There needed to be at least 5 articles to demonstrate the correct use of paragraph styles and character styles where necessary. I needed to use imagery, typography, colour and supporting design elements that would help convey the message and appeal to my chosen target audience.
