About me

Hi my name is Nick, I'm a home-based, freelance graphic designer, working in Wellington, New Zealand. I'm qualified with a Diploma of Computer Graphic Design from Natcoll Design Technology (Yoobee School of Design) with four years working experience. Please have a look around my portfolio and let me know if there's any way I can help you.

My services

I provide a graphic design service for the following:

  • Logos
  • Business cards
  • Letterheads
  • Compliment slips
  • Brochures
  • Flyers
  • Posters
  • Booklets
  • Catalogues
  • Photo manipulation

I don't do the printing or organise any printing. All final designs will be sent via email as a digital file to be used as is or as a print ready pdf for you to send to the printers.

Design process

  1. Contact me and let me know what you would like designed.

    2. Email me let me know what you would like me to design and when you would like the project to be completed, I work as an individual so I will need a reasonable amount of time to complete your project. Please be as detailed as possible and feel free to attach images, sketches, colour and typeface ideas, anything that will help me to understand what you would like me to design for you.

  2. I'll contact you and let you know when I can start.

    3. I’ll get back to you to let you know whether I can do the job and when I can get started. I might also require some more information from you concerning the project to understand it better.

  3. You will need to sign a contract.

    If you are happy with me doing the job for you and I’ve received all the information I need, I’ll send you a written brief and contract detailing the project. You will need to sign this contract and send it back to me before I start with your project. (print out the contract, sign it, scan it and send it back to me via email)

  4. I'll start developing the innitial concepts.

    5. Once I’ve received the signed contract, depending on what I'm designing for you, I’ll develop up to three rough innitial concepts; which I’ll send to you to get some feedback as to whether I’m on track with what you wanted and to get an idea of what you like and perhaps don't like from each concept.

  5. The final concept will be refined.

    6. When I get your feedback, I’ll take that information to produce one refined concept, which I’ll send back to you to review for any changes and eventually this will be used as a final solution.

  6. Final design will be produced.

    7. I’ll take the final concept and produce the final design and send it back to you as a proof for you to review, then you can get back to me with any small revisions you would like.

  7. Final design will be sent to you as a digital file.

    8. When you are happy with the final revisions made, the job will be signed off and I’ll send the final digital files, full payment will be expected whithin four weeks.

My pricing

I charge a casual rate of $50 per hour.
